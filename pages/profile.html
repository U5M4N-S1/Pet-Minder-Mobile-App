Terms & Privacy

How we keep PawPal safe for pets, owners and minders

Terms of Service

By using PawPal you agree to book and provide pet care responsibly. Owners must share accurate information about their pets, including health conditions and behaviour. Minders agree to care for each pet as if it were their own, follow the agreed schedule, and only accept bookings they are qualified to fulfil.

PawPal acts as a marketplace that connects owners with independent minders. Payments are processed through the app and released to the minder once a booking is marked complete. Cancellations and disputes are handled through the Help Centre.

Privacy Policy

We collect the details you give us at sign up — your name, email, location, pet profiles and (for minders) payout details — so we can match you with the right people and take payments. Messages sent through PawPal are stored so both parties can refer back to them, and review text is visible to other users.

We never sell your data. Card and bank details are stored in masked form and used only to process bookings and payouts. You can update or remove your information at any time from your profile, and deleting your account removes your personal data from active use.

Safety & Reporting

If something feels wrong — a missed visit, a safety concern, or behaviour that breaks our community rules — report it through the Help Centre. Our admin team reviews every report and can suspend or remove accounts that put pets or users at risk.

Last updated: April 2026. Questions? Use the Help Centre to get in touch.
